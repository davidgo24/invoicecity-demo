One place to send and track city invoices

Vendors submit invoices and pick which department should receive them. City staff see a queue by department and update status as they process each file.

How it works

1
Vendor submits Upload the PDF, choose the department (Transit, Fire, Finance, etc.), and enter amounts.
2
City reviews Staff open the file, check the PDF, and mark it in review or approved for payment.
3
Everyone tracks status Vendors see updates in their inbox. If something is wrong, the city returns it — vendor fixes and resubmits.
